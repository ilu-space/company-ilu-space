Common Questions

Things Worth Saying Plainly

You're a solo studio. Can you actually handle a full product build?
Yes — that's specifically what this is designed for. Many product builds are over-staffed with people who are coordinating rather than building. A single senior technical builder who can span the stack often moves faster and with fewer miscommunications than a team of specialists. For projects that need specific expertise (e.g. a specialist embedded engineer or a regulatory specialist), Ilu Space maintains a network of trusted collaborators who can be brought in scoped and cleanly.
How is this different from hiring a freelancer or a development agency?
A freelancer typically executes a defined brief. An agency manages a team executing your brief. Ilu Space operates as a technical co-founder — which means helping shape what to build, not just how to build it. The goal is to bring product judgment, not just execution. If you already know exactly what to build down to the spec, a freelancer is cheaper. If you're figuring out what AI product will actually move the needle for your business, this engagement model is more useful.
What does an AI Integration Sprint actually deliver?
A working, deployed system — not a report or a proof of concept that lives in a Jupyter notebook. Sprints typically start with a one-week diagnostic to identify the highest-leverage automation in your workflow, followed by two to three weeks of build and integration. You get working software in your stack by the end of it. The scope is intentionally bounded so it can actually be completed in the timeframe.
What is the Co-Built Ventures model and who is it for?
It's for people who have deep domain expertise and a real problem, but lack the technical capability to build a product. If you've spent 20 years in logistics and you know exactly where the inefficiency is but can't build the software yourself — that's the profile. Ilu Space brings the full technical build, and we structure an arrangement with shared economics appropriate to the contribution. These conversations happen on a case-by-case basis.
Do you work with companies outside Finland or the Nordics?
Yes. The Nordic market is a primary focus given the eldercare, climate, and industrial context — but the work itself is remote-compatible and the client base is intentionally global. If you're based in the EU, UK, or further afield and the problem is a good fit, geography is not a barrier.
What does engagement cost?
Pricing is scoped per project and varies considerably depending on scope, duration, and engagement type. AI Integration Sprints are bounded and priced as fixed-scope packages. Co-founder engagements and larger builds are typically structured with a monthly retainer plus milestone payments. The right starting point is a discovery call — there's no pressure and no pitch, just a direct conversation about whether there's a fit worth pursuing.
